Books:

I like to read a lot of books. But all of them fall into very few genres: Dystopia, (Auto)Biography (specifically of Physicists and Mathematicians), History of Science, and Epic Fantasy. I am trying to catalogue my favourite books at Gems and why I think they are great.

Movies:

My movie preferences are no different from that for Books. I enjoy watching Dystopia, Biography (specifically of Physicists and Mathematicians), Though I generally prefer Documentaries Over Movies. Just as is for books, I also list out my favorite movies and Documentaries at Gems.

Music:

I mostly have Gujarati Gazals playing through my earphones. With other kind of Music getting an occasional chance.

YouTube Channels and Blogs

Following are the reasons why I end up procrastinating on the internet for longer than expected:

  • xkcd: One of the best webcomics I know out there.
  • Veritasium: Really cool how Derek Muller explains stuff. eliminating misconceptions and not compromising with the details. There is this YouTube Channel and Website.
  • StandUp Maths: A YouTube Channel that is blend of Maths and StandUp Comedy.
  • 3 Blue 1 Brown: One of my favourite YouTube Channels. All videos are ~20 min long and almost always end up making me learn something new or see something from a different perspective.
